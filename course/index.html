The Power of Your Words:

Your prompt is the blueprint for your AI video. The quality and virality of your content hinge on your ability to tell the AI exactly what you want to see. Successful creators understand that precise, evocative language is key to generating videos that hook viewers instantly.

Conceptual Prompting Basics:

Think about describing your desired video in terms of:

  • **Main Subject:** What is the core object or scene?
  • **Action:** What is happening in the video?
  • **Visual Style/Mood:** What is the camera angle, lighting, or overall atmosphere?

By combining these elements, you begin to guide the AI toward your vision. Experimentation is crucial to seeing how small changes in your description can lead to big differences in the generated video.

Prompt Examples:

`A glass strawberry is sliced slowly with a knife. Close-up, dark background.`

`A thick pool of glowing lava is scooped with a spoon. Macro shot, warm lighting.`

`A stack of crystal pancakes is cut with a sharp knife. Black background.`

Trigger Word Cheat Sheet Preview:

Words like "macro," "cinematic lighting," "gooey," or "molten" can dramatically influence your AI's output.

`Camera Angles: close-up, macro, top-down, first-person`

`Lighting & Mood: cinematic lighting, soft light, glowing, dark background`

`Texture & Materials: jelly-like, stretchy, crystalline, glassy, molten, dripping`
